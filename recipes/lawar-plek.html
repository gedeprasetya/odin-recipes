Lawar Plek

Lawar Plek is a traditional Balinese dish made with minced meat, vegetables, and spices, often served as a side dish.

It is known for its unique flavor and is a staple in Balinese cuisine.

Lawar Plek is typically enjoyed with rice and other Balinese dishes.

This dish is often prepared during ceremonies and special occasions in Bali.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat (usually pork or chicken)
  • Grated coconut
  • Vegetables (such as green beans and carrots)
  • Spices (including garlic, shallots, and chili)

Steps

  1. Prepare the minced meat and cook it with spices until well done.
  2. Add grated coconut and mix thoroughly with the meat.
  3. Incorporate the vegetables and adjust seasoning as needed.
  4. Serve warm as a side dish with rice or other main courses.
