Bakso Balung

Bakso Balung is a traditional Indonesian meatball soup made with beef bones and a variety of spices.

This dish is known for its rich and savory flavor, often enjoyed with rice and sambal.

Bakso Balung is a popular street food in Indonesia, especially in Java.

Ingredients

  • Pork bones (for broth)
  • Ground pork
  • Garlic and shallots
  • Spices (such as nutmeg and pepper)
  • Rice noodles

Steps

  1. Prepare the pork bones and simmer them to make a rich broth.
  2. Mix ground pork with garlic, shallots, and spices to form meatballs.
  3. Cook the meatballs in the broth until they are fully cooked.
  4. Serve the soup with rice noodles and garnish with fresh herbs.
